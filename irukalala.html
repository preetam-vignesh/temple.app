Sri Irukalala Parameswari Ammavari Temple



Temple Name: Sri Irukalala Parameswari Ammavari Temple

History: Vikrama Simhapuri Village Goddess Sri Irukala Parameshwari Ammavari Temple is one of them. Kshetra Gadha says that this goddess, which is visited by many devotees not only locally but also from the surrounding districts, was established around the eighth century.
