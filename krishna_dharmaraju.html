Sri Krishna Dharmaraju Swamy Temple



Temple Name: Sri Krishna Dharmaraju Swamy Temple

History: Sri Krishna Dharmaraja Swamy Temple is located at Mulapet in Nellore city in the state of Andhra Pradesh. It is one of the ancient temples in Nellore city Draupadi Devi, Udhisthara, and Parthasarathy are the main deities in this temple. Brahmotsavams will be performed every three years in Jyeshta Masam for 21 days. Sri Jambu Maharshi had conducted a great Yagam a thousand years back here.
