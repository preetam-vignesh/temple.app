Golagamudi Temple



Temple Name: Golagamudi Temple

History: It is believed that while Garukmanth was bringing the 7 holy hills to Tirumala from Vaikunta in Kruta Yuga; a piece of the hills fell in Golagamudi. This was probably the reason for Swamy choosing Golagamudi as his final resting place. Today, lakhs of devotees visit his Samadhi in Golagamudi, the Shirdi of South.
